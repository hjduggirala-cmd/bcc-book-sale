üìñ Our Story

The B-CC Used Book Sale is a beloved community tradition that transforms donated books into educational opportunities. Every spring, hundreds of volunteers dedicate countless hours to collect, sort, and organize an incredible collection of books.

This massive event takes over much of the first floor of B-CC High School over two exciting days in March, creating a treasure hunt experience for book lovers of all ages.
